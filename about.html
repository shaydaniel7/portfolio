about



My name is Shay Daniel. I am from Baton Rouge, Louisiana. I earned my degree in elementary education from Louisiana State University and taught 4th grade for several years before moving overseas for mission work. Upon returning stateside, my husband Chad and I moved to St. Louis, Missouri, where we raised two boys. We moved to Redlands, California in 2007 and I currently work for Loma Linda University Children's Hospital as a medical program specialist at the Children's Assessment Center in north San Bernardino. I have been married for 29 years, our wonderful sons are grown, and we have two old, beloved pups. I enjoy photography, graphic design, hiking, traveling and being with my family.
